About Us

Jos Tech Girls was founded with the purpose of creating a colourful space in the male-dominated technology sector. Here, women can support each other, connect with one another and share their experiences and resources within a community of like-minded people.

Know us More

Vision

A Plateau where Women are equally represented across all career roles in Technology.

Mission

To build a community that embodies technical growth, networking, mentorship and visibility amongst all level career roles in Technology.

Core Values

Team work, Community, Technical growth,
Leadership, Visibility.

Tech has a new look

For years, tech excluded many of us. Things are changing. Tech is evolving. And Jos Tech Girls is here to steer it in the right direction..

If you love a welcoming comminity of women in tech like we do, we are more than excited to take you in.

Join other Women in Tech across our virtual community to network, stay updated, get help and grow in your career..
